The New Frontier

I study developmental pathways that regulate limb, heart, liver, and brain biology. My work focuses on understanding molecular mechanisms deeply enough to translate them into clearer models of disease, from dementia to cancer.

I integrate genomic, transcriptomic, and clinical data to identify how molecular programs regulate tissues across precise biological timepoints. The work calls for flexible modeling, pragmatic data integration, and the right tool for each frontier.
