The new frontier

I studied developmental pathways that regulate limb, heart, liver, and brain. I want to understand these mechanisms at the molecular level because we can solve diseases from dementia to cancer by looking at processes at the molecular level. To accomplish this, I integrated data to identify how these molecules regulate tissues at precise timepoints. I used whatever data is available and whatever model is appropriate. You can't be picky when exploring new frontiers.
