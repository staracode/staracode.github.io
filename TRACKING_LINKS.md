## GA4 Reports

In Google Analytics 4, check:

```text
Reports > Acquisition > Traffic acquisition
```

Useful dimensions:

```text
Session source / medium
Session campaign
```

Keep UTM values lowercase and consistent. GA treats `LinkedIn`, `linkedin`, and `linkedIn` as different values.
